Introduction

Goals & Features

The primary goal of Tide Tweet Metrics, designed for the University of Alabama's CS department, is to offer a comprehensive platform for analyzing and comparing Twitter posts against those of other universities. By providing insightful analytics and comparative data, we aim to enable the department to gain a deeper understanding of its social media performance in the context of peer institutions.

Dashboard

The Dashboard feature of Tide Tweet Metrics offers the University of Alabama's CS department a real-time view of its Twitter performance. Through intuitive navigation and comprehensive insights, the department can easily monitor key metrics and track engagement levels, facilitating informed decision-making and strategic adjustments to its social media strategy.

Metrics & Analysis

Tide Tweet Metrics provides the CS department with in-depth analysis capabilities to delve into the nuances of its Twitter presence. By exploring audience engagement, content performance, and comparative data with other universities, the department gains valuable insights to refine its communication tactics, identify trends, and capitalize on opportunities to enhance its online visibility and impact.

Custom Graph Generation

With Tide Tweet Metrics' Custom Graph Generation tool, the CS department can visually represent its Twitter data in tailored graphs and charts. This feature enables the department to illustrate trends, highlight successes, and compare its performance with that of peer institutions, providing a clear and compelling visualization of its social media efforts and accomplishments.

Automated Email Reports

Tide Tweet Metrics streamlines the monitoring process for the CS department through its Automated Email Reports feature. By delivering personalized summaries of key Twitter metrics directly to their inbox, department members stay informed and up-to-date on their social media performance.
